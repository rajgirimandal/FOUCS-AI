About Focus AI

Founder: Rajgiri Mandal

CEO: Rajgiri Mandal

What is Focus AI: A personal, browser-based AI assistant that monitors focus during Google Meet, online classes, and self-study.

How it works: Uses webcam and microphone streams with MoveNet and COCO-SSD locally in your browser to estimate focus and detect distractions/violations in real time.

Vision: Build a privacy-first Personal AI Focus Assistant that helps learners stay present without surveillance infrastructure.
